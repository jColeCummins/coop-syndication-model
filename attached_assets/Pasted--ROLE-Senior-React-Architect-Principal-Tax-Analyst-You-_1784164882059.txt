## NEW INPUT VARIABLES (Add to State)
- `noteTermYears`: Slider for the duration of the seller note (5 to 15 years, default 5). This dynamically alters the length of the financial tables.
- `stateTaxRate`: Slider for Ohio state income tax override (0% to 5%, step 0.1%, default 3.5%).
- `localTaxRate`: Slider for local municipal income tax override (0% - 3%, step 0.1%, default 1.5% for Yellow Springs).

## CALCULATIONS ENGINE UPGRADES

### 1. The Seller Cumulative Cash Flow Schedule
Generate an annualized data table showing the following columns: **Year, Gross Cash Received, Cumulative Post-Tax Cash In Pocket, Remaining Principal Balance**.
- **Year 0 (Day 1):** Gross Cash Received = `vacantLandCash` + `Down Payment Received`. Post-Tax Cash = Gross Cash minus the fully updated Year 1 IRS § 453(i) recapture tax and capital gains tax on the down payment.
- **Years 1 to `noteTermYears`:** For each year, calculate the annual P&I received. 
- **The Expiration Cliff Rule:** For Years 1 through 5, the ordinary income tax on the interest portion is shielded by the `cltLandDonation` value. For **Year 6 and beyond**, the `Charitable Shield` drops to ZERO, and the interest income is fully taxed at the seller's ordinary income rate plus the `stateTaxRate` and `localTaxRate` overrides. This must visibly demonstrate the financial penalty of extending the note past the 5-year carryforward window.
- **Final Year:** Add the `Balloon Payment` to the Gross Cash Received column.

### 2. The Investor Net Cash In/Out Table
Generate an annualized 5-year table detailing the cash flow velocity for the Phase 1 syndicate.
- **Columns:** Year, Capital Injected, Operating Cash Flow, Depreciation Tax Savings, Net Year-End Capital Position.
- **Year 0:** Capital Injected = `Down Payment Received` + Year 1 CapEx.
- **Year 1:** Depreciation Tax Savings = `Depreciation Basis` * 0.30 * (Investor's Ordinary Bracket + `stateTaxRate` + `localTaxRate`).
- **Year 5 (The Out):** Capital Injected = Negative value showing the return of original principal via the Co-op's Phase 2 refinance.

### 3. The Tenant Rent Cliff Projection
Show a side-by-side comparison card displaying Phase 1 Rent vs Phase 2 Rent (Year 6+), taking into account that the Phase 2 rent must cleanly support the commercial debt service required to pay off the remaining seller balloon and return the investors' capital.
