# ROLE: Principal Financial Architect & Senior React SPA Engineer

You are building an institutional-grade Real Estate Limited-Equity Co-op Syndication Dashboard. Your objective is to build a React Single Page Application (SPA) utilizing Tailwind CSS and Recharts (or rich HTML tables) that models the exact tax and cash flow friction points between a Seller, a Syndicate of Investors, and a Tenant Co-op.

## CRITICAL DIRECTIVES & ORDER OF OPERATIONS
Do NOT create circular dependencies. Follow this exact flow:
1. Read Global State.
2. Calculate Seller's Year 1 Tax Liability & Note parameters.
3. Calculate Investor Capital Requirements & MACRS Depreciation.
4. Calculate Tenant Revenue Required & Rent Deltas.
5. Render UI. 

Do NOT hallucinate tax law. Use the exact algebraic logic provided below.

## GLOBAL STATE (Variables & Sliders)

**Property & Tenant Baseline:**
- `units`: (Integer: 10 - 50, default 24)
- `currentRent`: ($400 - $1500, default $700)
- `vacancyRate`: (0% - 15%, step 1%, default 4%)
- `propertyTaxes`: ($10k - $50k, default $28472)
- `annualInsuranceMisc`: ($5k - $30k, default $15000)
- `mgmtFeePerDoor`: ($0 - $150, default $50)

**Seller's Financial Reality:**
- `totalFMV`: ($1M - $3M, step $50k, default $1.5M)
- `originalCostBasis`: 1993 Transferred Basis ($0 - $1M, step $10k, default $250k)
- `accumulatedDepreciation`: Total past depreciation taken by seller ($0 - $1M, step $10k, default $300k)
- `cltLandDonation`: Value of land donated to trust ($0 - $1M, step $10k, default $440k)

**The Deal Structure:**
- `sellerDownPaymentPct`: (0% - 30%, step 1%, default 15%)
- `sellerInterestRate`: (3% - 10%, step 0.25%, default 6%)
- `noteTermYears`: (5 - 30, step 1, default 20)
- `balloonYear`: (1 - 10, step 1, default 5)
- `phase2CommercialRate`: Year 6 Refinance Rate (4% - 10%, step 0.25%, default 7%)

**Investor New CapEx Buckets:**
- `capexRoofStruct`: 27.5-Year MACRS ($0 - $200k, default $90k)
- `capexParkingLand`: 15-Year MACRS ($0 - $100k, default $50k)
- `capexAppliances`: 5-Year MACRS ($0 - $100k, default $40k)

## THE ALGEBRAIC ENGINE

**1. The Seller's Crucible:**
- `Note Principal` = `totalFMV` - `cltLandDonation`
- `Down Payment Received` = `Note Principal` * (`sellerDownPaymentPct` / 100)
- `Adjusted Basis` = `originalCostBasis` - `accumulatedDepreciation`
- `Gross Profit %` = MAX(0, (`totalFMV` - `Adjusted Basis`) / `totalFMV`)
- `Year 1 Recapture Tax` = `accumulatedDepreciation` * 0.25
- `Year 1 CapGains Tax` = (`Down Payment Received` * `Gross Profit %`) * 0.15
- `Total Year 1 Tax Liability` = `Year 1 Recapture Tax` + `Year 1 CapGains Tax`
- **UI ALERT:** If `Total Year 1 Tax Liability` > `Down Payment Received`, flash a RED warning: "CRITICAL: Down Payment does not clear Year 1 IRS § 453(i) Recapture & CapGains."

**2. The Investor Profile (Capital & Yield):**
- `Total New CapEx` = `capexRoofStruct` + `capexParkingLand` + `capexAppliances`
- `Required Capital Injection` = `Down Payment Received` + `Total New CapEx`
- `Year 1 OBBBA Bonus Depreciation Shield` = `capexParkingLand` + `capexAppliances` + ((`totalFMV` * 0.75) * 0.30). *(Assumes 100% bonus on 5/15yr new CapEx, plus 30% segregated bonus on the acquired building).*
- **UI ALERT:** Note that passive investors cannot use this shield against W-2 income without IRC § 469(c)(7) REPS status.

**3. The Tenant Rent Cliff:**
- `Phase 1 Annual Revenue Req` = `calculatePMT(sellerInterestRate, noteTermYears, (Note Principal - Down Payment Received))` + `propertyTaxes` + `annualInsuranceMisc` + (`mgmtFeePerDoor` * `units` * 12).
- `Phase 1 Monthly Rent` = (`Phase 1 Annual Revenue Req` / `units` / 12) / (1 - (`vacancyRate` / 100)).
- `Rent Delta` = `Phase 1 Monthly Rent` - `currentRent`.
- `Phase 2 Refinance Burden` = Balloon balance at month (`balloonYear` * 12) + `Required Capital Injection`.
- `Phase 2 Monthly Rent` = Same formula as Phase 1, but swap the PMT to use `Phase 2 Refinance Burden` amortized over 30 years at `phase2CommercialRate`, and inflate mgmt fees by 15%.
- **UI ALERT:** If `Phase 2 Monthly Rent` > `Phase 1 Monthly Rent` by more than 10%, highlight Phase 2 Rent in RED.
